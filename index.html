About us

Aadya Duggal

Principal Designer

Aadya strongly believes that every project should be designed contextually evolving spaces that are perceived in new ways. Each design takes cognizes of the climate and integrates sustainability in cohesive way.

As she puts it, just like a good host my job is to anticipate the needs of my clients and I spend a lot of time and energy understanding their lifestyle and their habits.

Graduated from Sushant school of Design, Gurugram in 2018. She worked with Essentia Environments and The Workroom, Gurugram. Has a 5 years of work experience

Sanuj Gupta

Principal Designer

Sanuj is known for his simplicity in his design approach. Juxtaposes the orthogonal nature of clean lines with a luxurious mix of materials colors and texture in order to create a distinct look for each project.

His belief is that "design can be art, design can be aesthetics. good design is always simple and yet so complicated to execute." He also believes building spaces is a team effort.

Graduated from Arch college of Design and Business, Jaipur in 2019. He worked with BG associates and Yukti Interiors, Jaipur and The Workroom, Gurugram. Has a 4 years of work experience.
